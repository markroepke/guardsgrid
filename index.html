How to Play

Field a 3 × 3 lineup of Cleveland Guardians/Indians legends. Every player in the franchise's history (1901–present) is eligible. Same puzzle for everyone, every day — a new one drops at midnight.

Rules
  • Each cell needs a player who satisfies both the row and column criteria.
  • You can't repeat a player — once they're locked into one cell, they're off the board.
  • Tap any row or column label (or its i indicator) to see the full criteria description.
Cells
  • Cream — your player matches both criteria. Nice.
  • Red — your player doesn't satisfy at least one criterion. The cell tells you which.
  • Empty — you skipped this cell.
Endgame

When all 9 cells are filled the round ends automatically. Or hit Give Up any time. In the summary, tap any red or empty cell to see who would have worked.
